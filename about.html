This realisation sparked my interest in user experience design, prompting me to transition into the role of a product designer. I actively addressed users' challenges, reporting approximately 80 issues to various teams, including the product design team. My motivation is to create a product that satisfies both users and businesses. I aim to create a product that encourages users to return by focusing on the joy and convenience they experience while using the platform. Through this, I seek to attract new users and retain existing ones.
