About

Hello, I'm Choa, a UX designer with a background in marketing and customer service. My journey into UX design feels destined, shaped by a passion for creativity and problem-solving. When I was a customer service advisor, I discovered that users easily feel frustrated due to a single error message or the placement of a promotion box. This realisation sparked my interest in user experience design, prompting me to transition into the role of a product designer. I actively addressed users' challenges, reporting approximately 80 issues to various teams, including the product design team. One of the suggestions I made was to improve the visibility of the promotion code box, either by changing the wording to make it clearer or by using a more noticeable colour or button design. As a passionate UX designer, my goal is to contribute my e-commerce experience in the dog industry. As a huge dog lover, I aim to create intuitive and user-centric digital experiences for dog owners and enthusiasts. Beyond commerce, my aspirations extend to making a positive impact in the community. I am driven to contribute my skills to charitable and health-related projects, where I can apply my design capabilities to create meaningful and accessible solutions.
